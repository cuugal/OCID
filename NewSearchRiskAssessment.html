Risk Assessment

To create a NEW Risk Assessment:

  • enter an asterix, *, in the Chemical Name box to search all chemicals at your location,
  • select BOTH the Building and the Room to give the exact location of the chemical you wish to use,
  • then click 'Search Risk Assessment'.
To search for EXISTING Risk Assessments:
  • enter the Chemical Name (eg. * searches all assessments at the selected location, *sodium* searches for all chemicals containing the word 'sodium' at the selected location),
  • select the Location you wish to search,
  • then click 'Search Risk Assessment'.
NOTE: The wildcard character "*" may be used in the search text to match inexact chemical names.
"*" matches zero or more characters ( eg ben* matches benzene and benzoic acid).
Use "*" to search ALL CHEMICALS at a particular location.
